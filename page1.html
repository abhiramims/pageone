WELCOME TO ECE DEPARTMENT

VLSI LAB

The VLSI lab is well-equipped with some of the most modern facilities of recent times.Hardware equipment include FPGA development kits from Xilinx and Altera Inc. CoWare Tools, Xilinx ISEFoundation Software, VIVADO software and ZedboardZynq 7000 – Kits

ANALOG COMMUNICATION LAB

The Department has set up a sophisticated Communication Laboratory with the state-of-the-art equipment from Analog Devices and Texas Instruments. The laboratory is well equipped with hardware facilities consisting of Vector Network Analyser (6 GHz), Spectrum Analyzer (1.8 GHz), Synthesized RF Generators, Digital Storage Oscilloscopes (150-500 MHz).

EMBEDDED SYSTEMS LAB

Embedded Systems Laboratory is well equipped Laboratory which provides facilities for the design and development of microprocessor and micro-controller based systems which includes programming, debugging, testing and interfacing of various peripherals. Students can also do their Micro, Mini and Main projects based on various micro-controllers by using these facilities.

SIGNAL PROCESSING LAB

Digital Signal Processing (DSP) is concerned with the representation of signals in digital form, and with the transformation of such signal representations using digital computation. Digital Signal Processing is at the core of virtually all of today’s information technology, and its impact is felt everywhere — in telecommunications, medical technology, radar and sonar, and in seismic data analysis.

ELECTRONIC WORKSHOP

The Basic Electronics workshop is equipped with fundamental testing and measurement equipments such CRO, Function Generator, DC Power supply and Digital Multimeters. The laboratory is also equipped with Mobile phone trainer, Oscilloscope demonstrator trainer and transformer winding machine.

RF LAB

MITS has got an advanced and well equipped RF design laboratory covering design and verification of the concepts of modern digital communication systems that operates from KHz-GHz range. The lab includes experiments on applications of various communication protocols such as GSM/CDMA/WLAN/Bluetooth/Satellite/GPS etc., analysing different devices and components such as filters/amplifiers/antennas etc. which enables wireless communication effective, modulation techniques.

ABOUT
